Chicken Nugget Salad

Ingredients

  • 3 ounces frozen lightly breaded chicken nuggets
  • 4oz chopped lettuce
  • 6 cherry tomatoes, halved (3 ounces)
  • 3 teaspoons garlic powder
  • 8 teaspoons olive oil
  • 1 teaspoon honey
  • 1/4 cup croutons
  • 1 tablespoon white balsamic vinegar
  • 2 dashes black pepper
  • 1 bell pepper
  • 1/2 cucumber (or a small cucumber)
  • 1 carrot

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven according to the instructions on the frozen chicken nuggets packaging.
  2. Place the frozen lightly breaded chicken nuggets on a baking sheet. Brush 3 teaspoons of olive oil over the nuggets, then sprinkle garlic powder and a dash of black pepper. *(you can also add some chili pepper for extra flavor)*
  3. Bake the chicken nuggets in the preheated oven as per the package instructions.
  4. While the nuggets are baking, chop 4 ounces of lettuce.
  5. Add the sliced cucumber, carrot, cherry tomatoes, and bell pepper.
  6. In a mixing bowl, combine 5 teaspoons of olive oil, a dash of b lack pepper, 1 tablespoon of white balsamic vinegar, and 1 teaspoon of honey. Mix these ingredients together.
  7. Add the chopped lettuce to the bowl and toss it in the dressing.
  8. Once the chicken nuggets are done, place them on top of the dressed lettuce.
  9. Sprinkle 1/4 cup of croutons and gently toss the salad to combine all the ingredients.
  10. Serve immediately, and enjoy!
